URL Shortener Microservice

Example usage:

URL:
https://short-url-aedyan.herokuapp.com/short/https://www.google.com

Example output:

JSON:
{"original":"https://www.google.com","short":"https://short-url-aedyan.herokuapp.com/2800"}

Using:

Short URL:
https://short-url-aedyan.herokuapp.com/2800
Redirects to:
https://www.google.com
